Good questions

The things people ask first.

Is the first visit really free? +
Yes. Your first smile scan — the digital scan, a 3D preview of your future smile, and a written plan with pricing — is completely free, with zero obligation to begin.
How much will treatment actually cost? +
It depends on the treatment and your insurance, but our treatments start from $2,500, with most adult Invisalign and braces cases landing between $3,800 and $4,800. Use the cost calculator above for a realistic estimate, and you'll get an exact price at your free scan.
Do you take my insurance? +
We're in-network with most major plans (Aetna, BCBS, Cigna, Delta Dental, Guardian, MetLife, UnitedHealthcare and more) and we file every claim for you. Not listed? We'll file as an out-of-network provider.
Do you treat adults, or just teens? +
Both. Roughly half of our patients are adults. Discreet options like Invisalign and clear ceramic braces are built exactly for grown-up schedules and self-consciousness.
I'm nervous about appointments. Can you help? +
Absolutely — it's a big part of how we work. Dr. Park and the team specialize in calm, unhurried visits, and your first scan is a no-pressure conversation, not a sales pitch.
